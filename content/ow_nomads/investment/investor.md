# Become an co-founder in OurWorld Nomads

We are looking for co-founders in our project.

We are working on this for +3 years and have now access to beautiful properties.

## Our criteria are the following

- safety is our primary concern
- far away from any possible conflict 
- experiences, we are not just created places to be or work
    - we want to create places where we can experience energu and natural beauty.
- good enough Internet connectivity, its not always on those locations but it needs to be good enough.
- ability to get organic food or grow it ourselves
- good value for money
- places where digital nomads like to come
